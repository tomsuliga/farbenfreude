Farbenfreude Instructions

Phase 1 - Pick two gray tiles to reveal them. Then choose any visible black tile available. Points range from 1 to 8. This phase will last 10 rounds.

Phase 2 - Pick a single card from one of the three decks: Action, Fate, Mystery. Follow instructions on that card.

Action cards - deal with your specific points - generally changing the amount up or down.

Fate cards - involve you and your playing partners - generally trading or manipulating opponents.

Mystery cards - may result in chaos or supernatural events. Most people avoid these cards. Choose at your own risk.

Winner is first to get to 90 or more points.

Good Luck!
